United States renewable energy usage profile

Vu Duy Hieu, Nguyen Thanh An

The purpose of this project is to visualise the current situation of renewable energy usage by the United States; in particular, the trend of renewable energy usage among US states from 2000-2019, the usage flow of renewable energy in the Unites States, and the comparison of renewable energy use between the US and other countries.

We have built three visualisations for this project. The first visualisation is a tile grid map, with a stacked bar chart in each cell, which visualises the trend and comparison of renewable energy use among the states in the United States. The second visualisation is a Sankey chart that visualises the usage flow of different renewable energy sources. The third visualisation is a bubble chart that visualises the comparison of US renewable energy use profile with other countries based on different statistics such as land area or gdp.

Through the visualisations, we hope that our audience can learn interesting details about the US energy consumption trend, better understand the composition of America’s energy grid, and apply the knowledge to develop more and better renewable energy production and distribution systems to lessen the US’s reliance on fossil fuel and reducing the greenhouse gas effect.
