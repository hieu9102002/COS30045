Renewable energy usage profile by state in the US

The United States is very slowly consuming more and more percentage of its energy from renewable sources such as Hydropower and Solar energy. However, is this trend of renewable energy stagnation mirrored by the 50 states of the US?

We can explore the trend of renewable energy consumption of the United States and its states using the visualisation below, to see the percentage of renewable energy consumption of different sources relative to all energy consumption.
