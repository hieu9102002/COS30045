Usage of renewable energy

In this Sankey diagram we explore the total renewable energy consumption in the United States in 2019. Specifically, we illustrate the flow of different renewable energy sources into different use sectors.
The unit used to measure the energy flow is BBTUs (Billion British Thermal units). Users can also view the relative energy consumption between different sectors by clicking on the nodes.
